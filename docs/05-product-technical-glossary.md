# Product Technical Glossary

## Overview

This glossary defines technical terms used in product descriptions across all categories. It enables the AI assistant to explain specifications to customers, answer technical questions, and improve semantic search matching between customer queries and product listings.

---

## Electronics

**4K (Ultra HD)**
A display or camera resolution of approximately 3840 × 2160 pixels. Four times sharper than standard HD (1080p). Used in webcams, monitors, and dash cameras for high-detail capture.

**Bluetooth 5.0 / 5.3**
Wireless communication standard for short-range device pairing. Bluetooth 5.0+ offers 2× the speed and 4× the range of Bluetooth 4.2. Common in headphones, keyboards, and speakers.

**USB-C**
Universal connector standard supporting data transfer, power delivery (up to 240W), and video output. Replaces older USB-A and micro-USB connectors. Used in modern laptops, phones, and peripherals.

**Thunderbolt 4**
Intel's high-speed interface (40 Gbps) supporting USB-C connector. Enables daisy-chaining up to 6 devices, dual 4K monitor output, and fast data transfer. Required for high-end docking stations.

**NVMe (Non-Volatile Memory Express)**
A storage protocol for solid-state drives (SSDs) using PCIe lanes, offering speeds up to 7,000 MB/s — significantly faster than SATA SSDs (500 MB/s). Used in external drives and internal storage.

**ANC (Active Noise Cancellation)**
Technology that uses microphones to detect and counteract ambient sound in real-time. Reduces constant noises like airplane engines or HVAC systems. Different from passive noise isolation (physical blocking).

**Mechanical Keyboard Switch Types**
- **Linear (Red):** Smooth keystroke, quiet, preferred by gamers
- **Tactile (Brown):** Bump feedback without click, office-friendly
- **Clicky (Blue):** Audible click + tactile bump, typist favorite

**RGB**
Red-Green-Blue lighting system allowing millions of color combinations. Used in keyboards, mice, and PC components for aesthetic customization.

**HDMI 2.1**
Latest HDMI standard supporting up to 10K resolution, 4K@120Hz, and Variable Refresh Rate (VRR). Required for next-gen gaming monitors and TVs.

**IP Rating (IPX4, IP67, etc.)**
Ingress Protection rating defining resistance to solids (first digit) and liquids (second digit).
- **IPX4:** Splash-resistant
- **IP67:** Dust-tight + waterproof up to 1 meter for 30 minutes
- **IP68:** Dust-tight + waterproof beyond 1 meter

## Home & Kitchen

**Cast Iron**
Heavy cookware material with exceptional heat retention and even distribution. Requires seasoning (thin oil layer) to maintain non-stick properties. Lasts decades with proper care.

**Induction-Compatible**
Cookware that works on induction stovetops, which require magnetic materials. Stainless steel and cast iron are induction-compatible; aluminum and copper typically are not.

**BPA-Free**
Products free of Bisphenol A, a chemical found in some plastics that can leach into food. BPA-free is standard for food-contact plastics, bottles, and containers.

**Mandoline**
Kitchen slicing tool with adjustable blades for uniform cuts of vegetables and fruits. Enables julienne cuts, thin slices, and waffle cuts quickly.

**Cold Brew**
Coffee extraction method using cold water over 12–24 hours instead of hot water. Produces a smoother, less acidic concentrate. No heat required.

## Fitness

**Resistance Band Levels**
Bands are color-coded by resistance level:
- **Yellow:** Extra light (5–15 lbs)
- **Red:** Light (15–25 lbs)
- **Green:** Medium (25–65 lbs)
- **Blue:** Heavy (65–85 lbs)
- **Black:** Extra heavy (85+ lbs)

**Foam Roller Density**
- **Soft (white):** Beginners, gentle myofascial release
- **Medium (blue/pink):** Intermediate users
**Firm (black):** Advanced users, deep tissue work

**RPM (Revolutions Per Minute)**
Measurement used for jump ropes and cycling equipment. Higher RPM = faster rotation speed capability.

**Percussion Massager Amplitude**
The depth of each stroke a massage gun delivers (measured in mm). Higher amplitude (14–16mm) penetrates deeper muscle tissue than lower amplitude (10–12mm).

## Beauty & Personal Care

**Retinol**
Vitamin A derivative that accelerates skin cell turnover. Reduces fine lines, wrinkles, and hyperpigmentation. Can cause initial irritation — start with 0.25% and increase gradually.

**Hyaluronic Acid**
Humectant that attracts and holds up to 1,000× its weight in water. Plumps and hydrates skin. Effective at all concentrations (0.1%–2%).

**Niacinamide (Vitamin B3)**
Multi-purpose ingredient that minimizes pores, controls sebum, fades dark spots, and strengthens the skin barrier. Well-tolerated by most skin types. Effective at 5–10%.

**SPF (Sun Protection Factor)**
Measures UVB ray protection:
- **SPF 15:** Blocks ~93% of UVB
- **SPF 30:** Blocks ~97% of UVB
- **SPF 50:** Blocks ~98% of UVB

**AHA / BHA**
- **AHA (Alpha Hydroxy Acid):** Water-soluble exfoliant (glycolic, lactic acid). Works on skin surface. Ideal for dryness, dullness, fine lines.
- **BHA (Salicylic Acid):** Oil-soluble exfoliant. Penetrates pores. Ideal for acne and blackheads.

**Ceramides**
Lipid molecules naturally found in skin that maintain the moisture barrier. Ceramide-containing products repair dry, compromised, or eczema-prone skin.

## Food & Grocery

**MCT Oil (Medium-Chain Triglycerides)**
Fats derived from coconut oil that are rapidly absorbed and converted to energy. Popular in ketogenic diets. Available as oil or powder.

**Electrolytes**
Minerals (sodium, potassium, magnesium, calcium) that regulate fluid balance, muscle function, and nerve signaling. Lost through sweat — essential to replenish during/after exercise.

**CFU (Colony-Forming Units)**
Measurement of live bacteria in probiotic products. Higher CFU (10–50 billion) does not always mean better — strain diversity matters more.

**Cold-Pressed**
Extraction method using pressure (not heat) to preserve nutrients and flavor. Common in oils (olive, coconut) and juices.

**Nootropic**
Substance that supports cognitive function — memory, focus, creativity, or motivation. Examples: L-theanine, lion's mane mushroom, bacopa monnieri.

## Outdoor & Automotive

**Lumen**
Unit measuring total light output from a source. A standard candle ≈ 1 lumen. Tactical flashlights typically range from 300 (everyday carry) to 5,000+ lumens (search and rescue).

**PSI (Pounds per Square Inch)**
Pressure unit used for pressure washers and tire inflators. A 2,000 PSI pressure washer handles most residential cleaning; 3,000+ PSI for heavy-duty tasks.

**OBD2 (On-Board Diagnostics II)**
Standardized vehicle diagnostic system port (present in all US cars since 1996). OBD2 scanners read error codes, sensor data, and vehicle health metrics.

**IP67 / IP68 (Outdoor Context)**
For outdoor electronics (flashlights, dash cams), IP67 means protected against dust and water immersion up to 1 meter. IP68 allows deeper/longer submersion.

**Solar Panel Efficiency**
Percentage of sunlight converted to electricity. Consumer-grade solar garden lights typically operate at 15–22% efficiency. Higher efficiency = more power in low-light conditions.

## General E-Commerce Terms

**SKU (Stock Keeping Unit)**
Unique alphanumeric identifier for a specific product variant. Used internally for inventory management and customer support reference.

**MSRP (Manufacturer's Suggested Retail Price)**
The price a manufacturer recommends retailers charge. Actual sale price may differ.

**Bundle**
A set of complementary products sold together at a combined price, typically offering savings compared to buying each item individually.

**Lead Time**
Time between a customer placing an order and the item shipping. Differs from delivery time (transit time from warehouse to customer).

**Backorder**
An item that is temporarily out of stock but available for purchase. Order will ship when stock is replenished.
