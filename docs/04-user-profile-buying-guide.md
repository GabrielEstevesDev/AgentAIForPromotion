# Buying Guide by User Profile

## Overview

This guide provides personalized product recommendations based on customer profiles and lifestyle segments. The AI recommendation engine uses this document to tailor suggestions during browsing sessions and in response to natural language queries like *"what's good for a home office setup?"* or *"I'm looking for a gift for a fitness lover."*

---

## Profile 1 — The Remote Worker

### Profile Description
Works from home full-time or hybrid. Values productivity, comfort, and a clean desk setup. Frequently upgrades tech and ergonomics. High average order value.

### Pain Points
- Back and neck pain from poor ergonomics
- Video call audio/video quality
- Cable clutter and desk disorganization
- Distractions and focus issues

### Top Recommended Products

**Must-Haves:**
- Ergonomic Chair Lumbar Cushion *(Office Supplies)*
- Standing Desk Converter *(Office Supplies)*
- Monitor Riser *(Office Supplies)*
- USB Microphone *(Electronics)*
- 4K Webcam *(Electronics)*

**Nice-to-Haves:**
- USB-C Hub 7-in-1 *(Electronics)*
- Smart LED Desk Lamp *(Electronics)*
- Desk Pad Large 35x17 *(Office Supplies)*
- Cable Management Kit *(Electronics)*
- Noise-Cancelling Headphones *(Electronics)*

### Bundle Suggestion
> **"Home Office Pro Pack"** — Standing Desk Converter + Monitor Riser + Cable Management Kit + Desk Pad + USB-C Hub

### AI Prompt Triggers
*"work from home setup"*, *"home office upgrade"*, *"ergonomic desk"*, *"productivity tools"*, *"video call quality"*
