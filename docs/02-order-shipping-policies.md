# Order & Shipping Policies

## Overview

This document defines the complete lifecycle of an order on the platform — from placement to delivery — including shipping timelines, cancellation rules, return procedures, and refund policies. This content is used by the AI assistant to answer customer queries about their orders.

---

## Order Statuses

Every order on the platform follows a defined status lifecycle:

```
Pending → Shipped → Delivered
              ↓
          Cancelled
```

### Pending
- Order has been placed and payment confirmed
- Items are being prepared for shipment
- Customer can still cancel (see Cancellation Policy)
- Estimated duration: **1–3 business days**

### Shipped
- Order has left the warehouse
- Tracking number has been assigned and emailed to the customer
- No cancellation possible — return process applies instead
- Estimated duration: **2–7 business days** depending on destination

### Delivered
- Order confirmed delivered by carrier
- 30-day return window begins on delivery date
- Customer eligible to leave a review

### Cancelled
- Order cancelled before shipment
- Full refund issued within **3–5 business days**
- Stock automatically returned to inventory

---

## Shipping Options

### Standard Shipping
- Delivery in **5–7 business days**
- Free on orders over **$50**
- $4.99 flat rate for orders under $50

### Express Shipping
- Delivery in **2–3 business days**
- $9.99 flat rate regardless of order size
- Not available for oversized items

### Overnight Shipping
- Next business day delivery (order before 12:00 PM)
- $19.99 flat rate
- Available in continental US only

### International Shipping
- Available to 40+ countries
- Delivery in **10–21 business days**
- Customs duties and taxes are the buyer's responsibility
- Starting at $14.99 (varies by destination and weight)

## Cancellation Policy

### When Can You Cancel?

| Order Status | Cancellation Allowed? |
|---|---|
| Pending | ✅ Yes — full refund |
| Shipped | ❌ No — initiate a return instead |
| Delivered | ❌ No — initiate a return instead |
| Cancelled | — Already cancelled |

### How to Cancel
1. Log in to your account
2. Navigate to **My Orders**
3. Select the order and click **Cancel Order**
4. Confirm cancellation
5. Refund will be processed within 3–5 business days

> **Note:** Orders containing perishable or custom-made items cannot be cancelled once payment is confirmed.

---

## Returns Policy

### Return Window
- **30 days** from the confirmed delivery date
- Items must be unused, in original packaging, with tags attached

### Non-Returnable Items
- Perishable food and grocery products
- Opened beauty and personal care items
- Digital products and software licenses
- Customized or personalized products
- Hazardous materials

### How to Initiate a Return
1. Go to **My Orders** → select the order
2. Click **Return Item(s)** and select the reason
3. Print the prepaid return label (free for defective items; $5.99 deducted for change-of-mind returns)
4. Drop off at any affiliated carrier location within **7 days**
5. Refund processed within **5–7 business days** after warehouse receives the item

### Return Reasons
- Defective or damaged product
- Item not as described
- Wrong item received
- Changed mind / no longer needed
- Better price found elsewhere

## Refund Policy

### Refund Methods
Refunds are issued to the original payment method:

| Payment Method | Refund Timeline |
|---|---|
| Credit/Debit Card | 3–5 business days |
| PayPal | 1–3 business days |
| Store Credit | Instant |
| Bank Transfer | 5–10 business days |

### Partial Refunds
Partial refunds may be issued when:
- Item is returned with missing accessories or packaging
- Item shows signs of use beyond inspection
- Return is initiated after the 30-day window (discretionary)

### Refund Denied
A refund will be denied if:
- Item is not returned within the stated window
- Item is damaged due to customer misuse
- Item is in a non-returnable category

## Damaged or Incorrect Orders

If you received a damaged or incorrect item:
1. Contact support within **48 hours** of delivery
2. Provide photos of the damage/incorrect item
3. A replacement will be shipped within **2–3 business days** at no cost
4. You are **not required** to return the damaged item in most cases

---

## Order Tracking

- Tracking number is sent via email once the order is **Shipped**
- Track your order at any time in **My Orders → Track Shipment**
- Carriers used: UPS, FedEx, USPS, DHL (international)
- If tracking shows delivered but you haven't received it, wait 24 hours before contacting support (carrier delay)

## Frequently Asked Questions — Orders

**Q: My order has been Pending for more than 3 days. What should I do?**
A: Contact support. This may indicate a stock issue or payment verification delay.

**Q: Can I change my shipping address after placing an order?**
A: Only if the order is still in Pending status. Once shipped, address changes are not possible.

**Q: My order was delivered to the wrong address.**
A: If the address provided at checkout was correct, file a claim with the carrier. If an error was made at checkout, contact support for case-by-case resolution.

**Q: Can I combine two orders into one shipment?**
A: Order merging is not currently supported. Each order ships independently.

**Q: Do you ship to P.O. Boxes?**
A: Yes, for Standard and Economy shipping only. Express and Overnight shipping require a physical address.
