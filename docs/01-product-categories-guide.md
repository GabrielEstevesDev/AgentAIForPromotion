# Product Categories Guide

## Overview

This document provides detailed descriptions of all product categories available on the platform. Each category includes its core characteristics, target audience, key features to highlight, and associated keywords for semantic search and RAG retrieval.

---

## Electronics

### Description
Electronics encompasses personal tech devices, computer peripherals, audio equipment, and smart home gadgets. Products in this category are typically feature-driven purchases where specifications and compatibility are key decision factors.

### Target Audience
- Remote workers and students needing productivity gear
- Gamers and content creators
- Tech enthusiasts and early adopters

### Key Characteristics
- Spec-heavy product descriptions (resolution, frequency, connectivity)
- Compatibility with major operating systems (Windows, macOS, Linux)
- Warranty expectations: customers typically expect a minimum 1-year manufacturer warranty (this is an industry norm, NOT a platform guarantee — check individual product listings for actual warranty terms)
- High return rate if specs don't match expectations

### Top Product Types
- Headphones & audio (noise-cancelling, Bluetooth, wired)
- Input devices (keyboards, mice, webcams)
- Storage solutions (SSD, NVMe, external drives)
- Hubs, docks, and connectivity accessories
- Smart lighting and home automation

### Keywords
`wireless`, `bluetooth`, `USB-C`, `4K`, `noise-cancelling`, `mechanical`, `RGB`, `thunderbolt`, `HDMI`, `smart home`, `charging`, `portable`

## Home & Kitchen

### Description
Home & Kitchen covers cookware, kitchen appliances, organization tools, and sustainable home products. Buying decisions are often driven by durability, ease of cleaning, and brand reputation.

### Target Audience
- Home cooks and culinary enthusiasts
- Eco-conscious consumers
- New homeowners and apartment dwellers

### Key Characteristics
- Material matters: stainless steel, cast iron, ceramic, bamboo
- Ease of use and maintenance are primary concerns
- Sustainability and eco-friendliness increasingly important
- Gift-friendly category

### Top Product Types
- Cookware (skillets, woks, pots)
- Small appliances (air fryers, instant pots, coffee makers)
- Cutting and prep tools
- Storage and organization solutions
- Eco-friendly alternatives (beeswax wraps, compost bins)

### Keywords
`non-stick`, `dishwasher-safe`, `BPA-free`, `cast iron`, `bamboo`, `sustainable`, `meal prep`, `kitchen organization`, `eco-friendly`, `stainless steel`

## Fitness

### Description
Fitness products cover home gym equipment, recovery tools, and athletic accessories. This category sees strong seasonal spikes (New Year, summer) and benefits greatly from customer reviews and transformation stories.

### Target Audience
- Home gym enthusiasts
- Athletes and recreational sports players
- Beginners starting a fitness journey

### Key Characteristics
- Durability under repeated physical stress
- Weight and portability often important
- Customers rely heavily on reviews for sizing and resistance levels
- Bundles and sets perform well

### Top Product Types
- Free weights and resistance equipment
- Cardio accessories (jump ropes, rowing machines)
- Recovery tools (foam rollers, massage guns)
- Yoga and flexibility gear
- Athletic bags and accessories

### Keywords
`adjustable`, `resistance`, `recovery`, `portable`, `home gym`, `cardio`, `strength training`, `yoga`, `flexibility`, `muscle recovery`

- Students and lifelong learners
- Entrepreneurs and startup founders

- Self-help and personal development
- Technology and software engineering
- Psychology and behavioral economics
- Biography and memoir

### Keywords
`bestseller`, `productivity`, `mindset`, `entrepreneurship`, `leadership`, `software`, `system design`, `habit`, `philosophy`, `biography`

## Beauty & Personal Care

### Description
Beauty and personal care includes skincare, haircare, grooming, and wellness supplements. Ingredient transparency and dermatologist recommendations are major trust signals in this category.

### Target Audience
- Skincare enthusiasts and beginners
- Men's grooming market
- Health and wellness consumers

### Key Characteristics
- Ingredient lists are critical for informed buying
- Skin type compatibility (oily, dry, sensitive, combination)
- Cruelty-free and vegan claims increasingly valued
- Repeat purchase category with high loyalty

### Top Product Types
- Serums, moisturizers, and sunscreen
- Grooming kits and electric tools
- Hair oils and treatments
- Oral care
- Supplements (biotin, collagen, probiotics)

### Keywords
`vitamin C`, `retinol`, `hyaluronic acid`, `SPF`, `cruelty-free`, `vegan`, `dermatologist-tested`, `niacinamide`, `organic`, `collagen`

## Outdoor & Garden

### Description
Outdoor and garden products cover camping gear, gardening tools, lawn care, and outdoor living. Seasonality is strong — spring and summer drive gardening, while fall/winter drive camping and survival gear.

### Target Audience
- Gardening enthusiasts and urban farmers
- Hikers, campers, and outdoor adventurers
- Homeowners with yards and patios

### Key Characteristics
- Weather resistance and durability are non-negotiable
- Solar-powered and eco-friendly products growing fast
- Safety certifications matter for tools and lighting
- Seasonal demand peaks require inventory planning

### Top Product Types
- Camping and survival gear
- Garden beds, planters, and soil tools
- Power tools (leaf blowers, hedge trimmers)
- Outdoor lighting and fire pits
- Pest control and composting

### Keywords
`waterproof`, `solar-powered`, `durable`, `UV-resistant`, `cordless`, `composting`, `raised bed`, `camping`, `survival`, `outdoor living`

## Toys & Games

### Description
Toys and games include board games, educational kits, STEM toys, and creative play products. This category benefits from strong gifting cycles and the growing trend of adult board gaming.

### Target Audience
- Parents buying for children (ages 3–14)
- Adult gamers and hobbyists
- Gift buyers for birthdays and holidays

### Key Characteristics
- Age-appropriateness and safety ratings essential
- Educational value a growing purchase driver
- Replayability and social play valued in board games
- STEM toys see year-round demand, not just seasonal

### Top Product Types
- Building and construction sets (LEGO-style)
- Strategy and card games
- Science and experiment kits
- Creative arts (origami, kinetic sand)
- Remote-controlled and drone toys

### Keywords
`educational`, `STEM`, `ages 6+`, `strategy`, `family game night`, `replayable`, `non-toxic`, `science kit`, `creative play`, `building blocks`

## Pet Supplies

### Description
Pet supplies cover food accessories, health products, toys, and housing for dogs, cats, fish, birds, and small animals. Pet owners are highly loyal and research-driven buyers.

### Target Audience
- Dog and cat owners (primary market)
- Fish and reptile hobbyists
- Bird and small animal owners

### Key Characteristics
- Veterinarian recommendations carry significant weight
- Safety and non-toxic materials are non-negotiable
- Subscription and recurring purchases are common
- Personalization (size, breed) affects product fit

### Top Product Types
- Feeders and water systems
- Beds, carriers, and housing
- Grooming tools
- Training and enrichment toys
- Health supplements and probiotics

### Keywords
`non-toxic`, `vet-approved`, `washable`, `durable`, `interactive`, `GPS tracker`, `orthopedic`, `grain-free`, `calming`, `breed-specific`

## Automotive

### Description
Automotive accessories focus on in-car technology, maintenance tools, safety equipment, and interior upgrades. Buyers in this category prioritize compatibility with their vehicle make/model.

### Target Audience
- Car enthusiasts and DIY mechanics
- Daily commuters
- Road trip and long-distance drivers

### Key Characteristics
- Vehicle compatibility is the #1 concern
- Safety-critical products require certification (FCC, CE)
- Installation complexity affects customer satisfaction
- Reviews heavily influence trust for electronics and tools

### Top Product Types
- Dash cameras and GPS accessories
- Jump starters and emergency kits
- Interior organizers and seat accessories
- Diagnostic tools (OBD2 scanners)
- Detailing and cleaning products

### Keywords
`universal fit`, `dash cam`, `4K recording`, `OBD2`, `portable`, `roadside emergency`, `interior`, `waterproof`, `easy install`, `night vision`
