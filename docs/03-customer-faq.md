# Customer FAQ — E-Commerce Platform

## Overview

This document contains 35+ frequently asked questions organized by topic. It is the primary reference for the AI assistant when answering customer support queries.

---

## Account & Registration

**Q1: How do I create an account?**
A: Click **Sign Up** on the homepage, enter your first name, last name, email address, and a password. You'll receive a confirmation email — click the link to activate your account.

**Q2: I forgot my password. How do I reset it?**
A: On the login page, click **Forgot Password**, enter your registered email, and follow the link sent to your inbox. The reset link expires after 24 hours.

**Q3: Can I use the platform without creating an account?**
A: You can browse and search products without an account, but checkout requires registration to track your orders and manage returns.

**Q4: How do I update my email address or phone number?**
A: Go to **My Account → Profile Settings** and update your contact information. A verification email will be sent to confirm the new address.

**Q5: Can I have multiple accounts with the same email?**
A: No. Each email address is unique per account. If you receive an error, you may already have an account — try the password reset flow.

**Q6: How do I delete my account?**
A: Go to **My Account → Privacy Settings → Delete Account**. This action is permanent and removes all your data including order history and reviews.

## Orders

**Q7: How do I place an order?**
A: Add items to your cart, proceed to checkout, enter your shipping address and payment details, and confirm your order. You'll receive an order confirmation email immediately.

**Q8: Can I modify an order after placing it?**
A: Order modifications are only possible while the status is **Pending**. You cannot change items, quantities, or shipping address once the order is **Shipped**.

**Q9: How do I cancel an order?**
A: Go to **My Orders**, select the order, and click **Cancel Order**. This is only available for orders with **Pending** status.

**Q10: Why was my order cancelled automatically?**
A: Automatic cancellations occur if payment fails verification, the item goes out of stock after purchase, or there is a shipping restriction to your address.

**Q11: What does "Pending" status mean?**
A: Your order has been received and payment confirmed. The warehouse is preparing your items. This typically takes 1–3 business days.

**Q12: What does "Shipped" status mean?**
A: Your package has left the warehouse and is with the carrier. Check your email for the tracking number.

**Q13: What does "Delivered" status mean?**
A: The carrier has marked your package as delivered. If you haven't received it, check with neighbors or building reception, then contact support.

**Q14: Can I reorder a previous purchase?**
A: Yes. Go to **My Orders**, find the order, and click **Reorder**. Items will be added to your cart at current prices.

**Q15: I placed two orders. Can they be combined into one shipment?**
A: Order consolidation is not supported at this time. Each order ships independently.

## Payments

**Q16: What payment methods do you accept?**
A: We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. Bank transfers are available for orders over $200.

**Q17: Is my payment information secure?**
A: Yes. We use PCI DSS-compliant encryption. We never store your full card number on our servers.

**Q18: My payment was declined. What should I do?**
A: Check that your card details are correct, your billing address matches your bank records, and that you have sufficient funds. If the issue persists, try a different payment method or contact your bank.

**Q19: Was I charged twice for one order?**
A: You may see a temporary authorization hold alongside the actual charge. The hold releases within 3–5 business days. If you see two identical charges, contact support immediately.

**Q20: Can I split payment between multiple methods?**
A: Not at this time. Only one payment method can be used per order. Store credit can be applied alongside a primary payment method.

## Shipping & Delivery

**Q21: How long does standard shipping take?**
A: Standard shipping delivers within **5–7 business days** for domestic orders.

**Q22: Is free shipping available?**
A: Yes — free standard shipping on all orders over **$50**.

**Q23: Do you ship internationally?**
A: Yes, to 40+ countries. International delivery takes 10–21 business days. Customs duties may apply.

**Q24: My tracking hasn't updated in several days. Is my package lost?**
A: Tracking gaps of 2–3 days are normal during transit. If there's been no update for **5+ business days**, contact support and we'll file a carrier inquiry.

**Q25: The tracking says delivered but I didn't receive my package.**
A: Wait 24 hours (carrier scans can be early). Check with neighbors, your building reception, or any safe spots. If still not found after 24 hours, contact support.

## Returns & Refunds

**Q26: How long do I have to return an item?**
A: **30 days** from the confirmed delivery date.

**Q27: How do I return an item?**
A: Go to **My Orders → Return Item**, select your reason, and print the return label. Drop the package at any affiliated carrier location within 7 days.

**Q28: Do I have to pay for return shipping?**
A: Return shipping is **free for defective or incorrect items**. For change-of-mind returns, a $5.99 return shipping fee is deducted from your refund.

**Q29: When will I receive my refund?**
A: After the warehouse receives and inspects your return (3–5 business days), your refund is processed within 3–5 additional business days to your original payment method.

**Q30: Can I exchange an item instead of returning it?**
A: Direct exchanges are not available. Return your item for a refund and place a new order for the replacement.

## Products & Inventory

**Q31: How do I know if a product is in stock?**
A: The product page shows real-time stock status. If an item shows **Out of Stock**, you can sign up for a restock notification.

**Q32: Are product descriptions accurate?**
A: We work with suppliers to ensure accuracy, but slight variations in color may exist due to screen calibration differences.

**Q33: Can I request a product that isn't listed?**
A: Use the **Product Request** form in the Help Center. Our purchasing team reviews requests monthly.

**Q34: What does the SKU number mean?**
A: The SKU (Stock Keeping Unit) is a unique identifier for each product. Use it when contacting support to ensure we reference the exact item.

## Reviews

**Q35: How do I leave a review?**
A: After your order status reaches **Delivered**, go to **My Orders**, select the item, and click **Leave a Review**. Ratings are 1–5 stars with an optional comment.

**Q36: Can I edit or delete my review?**
A: You can edit your review within **14 days** of posting. Reviews cannot be deleted by the customer — contact support if you need a review removed.

**Q37: Why was my review not published?**
A: Reviews are moderated for spam, hate speech, and off-topic content. If your review was rejected, you'll receive an email with the reason.

## AI Assistant

**Q38: What can the AI assistant help me with?**
A: The AI assistant can help you find products, track orders, answer policy questions, compare items, and get personalized recommendations.

**Q39: Can the AI assistant process a return or cancel an order?**
A: The AI can guide you through the steps, but account-specific actions (cancellations, refunds) require confirmation through your account portal or human support.

**Q40: Is my conversation with the AI stored?**
A: Conversations may be stored for quality improvement purposes. No payment or sensitive personal data is processed through the chat interface.
